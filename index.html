🚀 Accessing Your Amplify Classroom

Please follow the steps below based on whether this is your first time logging in or you are returning to Amplify.

🔰 First-Time Login Instructions (Use CAVA Google Account)

  1. Log into your CAVA Google account.
    This is your school account that ends in @californiava.org or @californiavirtualacademy.org.
  2. Click the link your teacher gave you to join the Amplify class.
    The class join code will be automatically filled in.
  3. Click “Log in to Amplify”.
  4. Click “Log in with Google” and select your CAVA Google account.
  5. ✅ You’re now enrolled and ready to begin using Amplify!
💡 Why use your Google account the first time?
This ensures your name appears correctly and uniformly in your teacher's dashboard.

🔁 Returning Login Instructions (Use ClassLink)

  1. Make sure you're still logged into your CAVA Google account.
  2. Go to the ClassLink portal:
    👉 launchpad.classlink.com/californiavirtualacademies
  3. Log in using your school Google credentials if prompted.
  4. Click the Amplify icon in your ClassLink dashboard.
  5. ✅ You’re now back in your Amplify Classroom and ready to continue learning!
⚠️ Troubleshooting Tips
• Always use your CAVA Google account (never a personal Gmail).
• First login must go through Google directly to set your account name correctly.
• After that, you can use ClassLink for quick access.
• If you see errors, try logging out of all accounts and starting over.

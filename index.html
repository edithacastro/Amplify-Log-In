🚀 Accessing Your Amplify Classroom

Follow the instructions below depending on whether you're logging in for the first time or returning to Amplify.

🔰 First-Time Login Instructions

  1. Log into your CAVA Google account.
    This is your school account that ends in @californiava.org.
  2. Click the link your teacher gave you to join the Amplify class.
    The class join code will be automatically filled in.
  3. Click “Log in to Amplify”.
  4. Click “Log in with Google” and select your CAVA Google account.
  5. ✅ You’re now enrolled and ready to begin using Amplify!
💡 Why is this important?
Using your @californiava.org account ensures your name is stored correctly and consistently for your teacher in the Amplify dashboard.

🔁 Returning Login Instructions

  1. Log into your CAVA Google account first — always use your school-issued account ending in @californiava.org.
  2. Go to 👉 https://my.amplify.com
  3. Click “Log in with Google” and choose your CAVA Google account.
  4. ✅ You’ll be logged in and can continue working on your Amplify activities.
⚠️ Troubleshooting Tips
• Make sure you're using your @californiava.org account — not a personal Gmail.
• If Amplify shows an error or blank screen, log out of all Google accounts and sign in again using your CAVA Google account only.
• Bookmark https://my.amplify.com for easy access.
